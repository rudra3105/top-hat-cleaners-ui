Saree Cleaning Requires Special Care

Sarees cannot be treated like regular garments. Many sarees include delicate materials and decorative work that require specialized inspection and cleaning methods.

Many sarees include:

Silk fabric
Hand embroidery
Zari borders
Beadwork
Stonework
Sequins
Pleats
Delicate dye
Designer finishing
Heavy borders
Attached blouse pieces
⚠️ Improper cleaning may cause fading, bleeding, shrinkage, fabric damage, or loss of shape. That is why specialty inspection matters before cleaning begins.
At Top Hat Cleaners, we review the garment carefully and recommend the safest cleaning method based on fabric, decoration, age, condition, and stain type.
