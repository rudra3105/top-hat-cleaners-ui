Why Dry Cleaning Prices Are Different for Every Garment

Not all garments can be cleaned the same way. For example:

  • A cotton dress shirt is very different from a silk saree.
  • A business suit requires shaping and pressing.
  • Wedding garments often include delicate fabrics, beadwork, and embroidery.
  • Comforters need larger equipment and longer drying cycles.

Professional cleaners inspect garments carefully before cleaning to determine the safest and most effective process.

This is one reason why specialty garments usually cost more.
